FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is CruiseboXx?

CruiseboXx is an event technology service that provides you with fun events.

How does CruiseboXx work?

It's so easy! All you have to do is register on our website or app and begin your search for fun events.

How do I get started?

i)Create a profile by signing up on the dashboard with your email and password.
ii)Verify your account with the verification code sent to your email address.

Can I use CruiseBoXx for my event and ticket registration?

Yes. CruiseboXx is basically an event creation and discovery software. You can register your events using the following steps;
i)Go to settings
ii)Click on manage your event
iii)Select 'create new event'
iv)Click on 'online' to enhance the visibility of your event.
v)Click on 'save changes' and update the conent.

How can I promote my event?

Events can be promoted by simply using the CruiseboXx marketing tools such as offering promotions,selling tickets at discounted rates etc.

How much does CruiseBoXx cost?

It is free for individuals. However, businesses that want to create events would be priced. This pricing varies according to expectations.Contact our customer care for more details.

Can I buy tickets to events on CruiseboXx?

Yes. Event tickets can be purchased on the online event page as registered by the organizers.

What happens if my ticket gets missing?

Tickets can only be declared missing if the information provided during the application process differs.It could also be disabled by the organizers.

I can’t find any events in my city, why?

CruiseboXx doesn't operate in locations. It currently operates in Lagos only.

What does it mean to discover an event?

Discovering an event is simply searching for an event according to yourpreferences, either by location or budget.

Can I get a ticket fee refund for an event that did not occur?

We verify every event before putting it on our platform. However, if the events don't hold anymore, kindly contact the event organizers should you have any questions about their refund policy.

Can I use the CruiseBoXx app if it is not in my city?

No. It currently operates in Lagos only. We would announce it if we extend our reach to other locations.

Can I change my account settings?

Account settings can be changed at any time but users must ensure to use correct details for event registration and ticket reservations.

Why does the app ask me to turn on location services?

This is simply to help you find your desired events wherever you are.

Are the fun events affordable?

Relatively, yes. You have the option of searching for events according to your budget.

Is my safety guaranteed in these event venues?

We don't display events in unsafe places. However, we are not responsible for any unforeseen circumstances you might face.

Who sees the events that I create?

Every newly added event automatically shows on a default page which is visible to everyone who visits CruiseboXx.

How can I get in touch with the CruiseBoXx team?

Buzz us at: info@CruiseBoXx.com.
